History

Abbey Physiotherapy (formerly Ailbe Physiotherapy) was established in Clane, Co. Kildare in 2008. In 2015, we began offering personal training, yoga and pilates in our new expanded premises. All our physiotherapists are all members of the Irish Society of Chartered Physiotherapists and we take pride in providing the highest quality care for our patients. Our personal trainers and yoga/pilates teachers are all also fully certified. For more information on our full range of services please click here.

Paul Murphy

Head Physiotherapist & Clinic Owner

Our head physio and founder, Paul, has over 17 years experience in providing quality care to patients in both public and private practice. Paul qualified with a Bachelor of Physiotherapy from UCD in 2002 before continuing to complete a Masters of Physiotherapy in 2003. After 4 years of experience working in the Mater Hospital Dublin Paul undertook a second Masters in the University of Queensland, Brisbane, Australia. Upon returning home to Ireland, Paul worked for a number of private practices before founding his own practice in 2008. Paul is chartered Physiotherapist and regularly attends courses and conferences both internationally and at home.
